PARC: Alignment

The Home Depot

I felt that the Home Depot did a good job of alignment. They have ther columns reduced so that you can find items under a given topic, like my image of maintenance essestials. This allows us to select the area we need without continuing to scroll.

PARC: Contrast

The Church of Jesus Christ

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints home page is a great example of contrast. They make sure you can read everything clearly and with contrasting colors from images and text. When the image is dark the text is white and when words on a white background the text color is black.
